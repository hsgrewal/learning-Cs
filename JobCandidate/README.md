# Job Candidate

A C# program that asks a job candidate for their years of experience. Then, it
categorizes the candidate into the following experience levels:

| Years of Experience | Experience Level |
|:-------------------:|:----------------:|
|         0           | Inexperienced    |
|         1           | Junior           |
|         2           | Intermediate     |
|         3           | Advanced         |
|         4+          | Senior           |
